About ColorStack

Increase the entrance, retention, and success of all UNC Charlotte students pursuing careers in computing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ColorStack?
ColorStack is a student organization dedicated to increasing the entrance, retention, and success of students pursuing careers in computing.

How can I join ColorStack?
You can join ColorStack by reaching out to our team on campus, attending an interest meeting, or signing up on our official website.

What events does ColorStack host?
ColorStack hosts events such as coding workshops, networking opportunities, interview prep sessions, hackathons, and more.

Who can join ColorStack?
ColorStack is open to all students interested in computing, regardless of major or background.

Does ColorStack provide career support?
Yes! We provide career support through mock interviews, resume reviews, and partnerships with companies in the tech industry.

How can I stay updated on ColorStack activities?
Follow us on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for the latest updates on ColorStack activities and events.
